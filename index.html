AK47

The AK-47, officially known as the Kalashnikov, is a highly renowned and durable assault rifle developed by Mikhail Kalashnikov. Designed in 1947, it features a gas-operated, selective-fire mechanism with a reputation for reliability in harsh conditions. Its simplicity, ruggedness, and ease of use have made it a favored choice for armed forces and militias worldwide. The AK-47’s distinctive curved magazine and iconic design have also made it a symbol in both military and cultural contexts, representing both strength and controversy.
